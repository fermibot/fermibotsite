This interactive timeline explores Gattaca (1997), Andrew Niccol's dystopian vision of a future where genetic perfection determines destiny. Vincent Freeman, an "In-Valid" born naturally with genetic imperfections, assumes the identity of Jerome Morrow, a genetically superior "Valid," to pursue his dream of space travel. The visualization tracks Vincent's daily deception, the mounting investigation that threatens to expose him, and his ultimate triumph of determination over biology.
