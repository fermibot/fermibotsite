  • Crabeater Seal is the most numerous seal species (~15 million)

  • Baikal Seal is the only exclusively freshwater seal species
  • Elephant Seals are the largest pinnipeds (up to 5m long)
  • Leopard Seals are apex predators that hunt penguins

  • Caspian Seal: Population declined 90% since 1900 due to hunting and pollution.
  • Australian Sea Lion: Declining due to fisheries bycatch and habitat disturbance.
